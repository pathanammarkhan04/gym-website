Personalized Diet Plan

A simple, balanced weekly diet to support fitness goals. Adjust portions to match your calorie target and activity level.

Daily Meal Template

  • Breakfast: Protein + complex carbs + fruit (e.g., oats with whey and berries)
  • Mid-morning snack: Nuts or Greek yogurt
  • Lunch: Lean protein + veggies + brown rice / whole grain
  • Afternoon snack: Veggies and hummus or a protein shake
  • Dinner: Lean protein + salad/steamed vegetables
  • Optional: Small evening snack if needed (cottage cheese or casein)

Quick Tips

  • Stay hydrated: aim for 2–3L water daily.
  • Prioritize whole foods and minimize processed items.
  • Adjust portion sizes to meet your calorie goals.
  • Include a protein source at every meal.
